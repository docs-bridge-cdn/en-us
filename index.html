Advanced Trézor Hardware Security

Military-Grade Security Architecture

Trézor sets the industry standard with its advanced security architecture, incorporating a secure element chip certified to CC EAL5+. This sophisticated hardware ensures your private keys remain completely isolated from potential online threats. Every transaction requires physical verification through the device's built-in display and buttons, creating an impenetrable air-gapped security system that prevents remote attacks and unauthorized access attempts.

Innovative Recovery System

Experience peace of mind with Trézor's revolutionary recovery system. The wallet implements the BIP39 standard, generating a secure 24-word recovery seed that serves as your master key. This seed can restore your entire wallet across multiple devices, ensuring you never lose access to your assets. The innovative Shamir Backup feature splits your recovery seed into multiple shares, requiring a predetermined number for restoration, eliminating single points of failure.

Universal Cryptocurrency Support

Manage your diverse crypto portfolio with unparalleled convenience. Trézor supports over 1,000 cryptocurrencies, from major coins like Bitcoin and Ethereum to a wide range of altcoins and tokens. The intuitive Trézor Suite software provides real-time portfolio management, direct exchange access, and detailed transaction history while maintaining absolute security. Our open-source approach ensures transparency and continuous security auditing by the community.

Advanced Protection Features

Beyond basic security, Trézor offers cutting-edge protection features. The passphrase protection enables hidden wallets for enhanced privacy, while the tamper-evident packaging and secure boot mechanism safeguard against physical tampering. The device's dual-chip design creates true isolation between sensitive operations and potential attack vectors, ensuring your private keys never leave the secure environment, even during firmware updates or system recoveries.
